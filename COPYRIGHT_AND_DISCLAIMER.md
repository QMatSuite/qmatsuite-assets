**Source**: PseudoDojo Project  
**Upstream URLs**:
- https://www.pseudo-dojo.org
- https://www.pseudo-dojo.org/faq.html

**Attribution**: PseudoDojo is a collaborative project providing pseudopotentials for Quantum ESPRESSO. The library includes:
- ONCVPSP (Optimized Norm-Conserving Vanderbilt Pseudopotentials) by D. R. Hamann
- PAW (Projector Augmented Wave) pseudopotentials from the JTH (Jollet-Torrent-Holzwarth) library

Please refer to the PseudoDojo website for citation information and license terms.

**Attribution**: These pseudopotentials were developed by Davide Ceresoli for GIPAW (Gauge Including Projector Augmented Wave) calculations. Please refer to the upstream page for citation information and license terms.

**Upstream URL**: https://yaoyi92.github.io/scan-tm-pseudopotentials.html
